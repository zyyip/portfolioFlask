Reason

Why did i took this course.

Right after graduating from university, an epidemic occured and there was a lockdown in Singapore. As a fresh graduate, I was stuck in my progression and I felt kind of lost in what should I do next and actually chanced upon edX.

edX has a wide range of courses to choose from and I saw that edX had online courses on Python Web Development. I always liked developing websites and mobile applications, I felt that I could take this opportunity to learn another language which is currently quite popular due to its ease of use.

The course was quite challenging and it took a longer time to complete than expected due to the number of projects given. However, I felt that the instructor(Brian) way of teaching was excellent. He gave working examples and his instructions were very clear and I had a great time listening to his lecture.
