Final Year Project

University

Android OS as an open sourced OS, which allows user to install any apps even those that are not from the google play store.

This action can impose some security issues.

The purpose of this project is to the following:

 1. Keep track on all the apps that have been installed "directly" via the APK

 2. Monitor the behaviour of the apps that are listen from step 1

The aim is to monitor if there exists any behaviour change in the system, such as unauthorised installation of other apps, or any other things that can be categorized as malicious software.

Final Year Project

Polytechnic

Participated in the IBM BlueMix Challenge for my polytechnic final year project. I was tasked to develop an application based off a self-thought innovative concept that had to use IBM Cloud services.

The idea was to develop an interactive android health and fitness application that allowed users to communicate with their families and friends to plan exercising routines on a daily basis. The app also provided basic functionalities such as, calculating and planning of the amount of calories to be reduced and the ability to navigate throughout the world with google maps sdk.

IBM liked our concept and we were chosen as the top 5 finalists group amongst 20 other groups that has participated in the challenge.
