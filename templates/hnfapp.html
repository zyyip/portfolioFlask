Summary

What is this project about.

I was tasked to develop an application bsed off a self-thought innovative concept that had to use IBM Cloud services.

The idea was to develop an interactive android health and btness application that allowed users to communicate with their families and friends to plan exercising routines on a daily basis. The app also provided basic functionalities such as, calculating and planning of the amount of calories to be reduced and the ability to navigate throughout the world with google maps sdk.

IBM liked our concept and we were chosen as the top 5 finalists group amongst 20 other groups that has participated in the challenge.
